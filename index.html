Graphic Design Projects

Adobe Photoshop - Advertisement

For my graphic design class, I undertook the task of crafting a lifelike poster advertisement using Adobe Photoshop. The challenge was to showcase any random object in a realistic manner. I opted for a piece of Ghirardelli chocolate as my subject. The constraints were clear: rely solely on Adobe Photoshop functions to bring the advertisement to life.

Autodesk Inventor - Reusable Water Bottle

This water bottle design originated from an engineering competition where participants were tasked with creating a 3D model of any object, such as a machine part, tool, or device. I opted to develop a representation of a reusable water bottle, and the design successfully made it to the finalist stage in the competition.

Adobe Photoshop - Movie Poster

For a graphic design class assignment, I was tasked with crafting a movie poster using Adobe Photoshop features exclusively. The unique twist was that the movie name was generated randomly. This challenge pushed my creativity as I had to rely solely on the tools within Adobe Photoshop.
